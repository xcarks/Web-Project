Research Themes

Our Focus Areas

Click a theme to explore the research being conducted, key publications, and opportunities for collaboration.

Trustworthy & Explainable AI

+
How can AI systems be made transparent, fair, and reliable? Our researchers are developing interpretability methods, bias detection tools, and formal verification frameworks for machine learning models used in critical applications like healthcare and criminal justice.

Quantum Computing & Post-Quantum Cryptography

+
As quantum computers approach practical utility, our cryptography researchers are developing post-quantum algorithms resistant to quantum attacks. We also explore quantum machine learning and simulation algorithms in collaboration with IBM Quantum and CEA.

Privacy-Preserving Data Science

+
Techniques such as differential privacy, federated learning, and homomorphic encryption allow data analysis without exposing sensitive information. Our DATALABS team is pioneering practical deployments in healthcare and financial services.

Edge AI & Embedded Intelligence

+
Running AI inference on resource-constrained devices is a key challenge for IoT, autonomous vehicles, and smart cities. Our teams develop model compression, hardware-aware neural architecture search, and runtime optimization techniques.

Human-Computer Interaction & Accessibility

+
We study how humans interact with complex digital systems and design interfaces that are inclusive, intuitive, and empowering. This includes research on voice interfaces, brain-computer interfaces, and tools for users with disabilities.
