FAQ

Common Questions

When do applications open for the next academic year?

+
Applications for the 2025–2026 academic year open in October 2024 on Parcoursup (for bachelor's level) and directly on the EFREI website for Master's programs. We strongly recommend applying early as competitive programs fill quickly.

What are the language requirements for international applicants?

+
For French-language programs, a minimum DELF B2 (or equivalent) is required. For English-taught Master's programs, a TOEFL iBT score of 90+ or IELTS 6.5+ is expected. Non-native English speakers must provide official test scores.

Is there financial aid available?

+
Yes. EFREI offers merit scholarships covering 20–100% of tuition fees, French government grants (CROUS), and social scholarships. The apprenticeship program also allows students to offset tuition costs with their salary. Contact admissions for a personalized assessment.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

+
Absolutely! We organize open days (Journées Portes Ouvertes) in November, January, and March. Individual campus tours can also be arranged by appointment. Online virtual tours are available year-round on our website.

How do I find an apprenticeship host company?

+
EFREI's Career Center actively supports students in their apprenticeship search. We organize dedicated "Alternance Fair" events, provide a curated job board, and connect students with our 120+ partner companies. The school does not guarantee placement, but our support team has a strong track record.
