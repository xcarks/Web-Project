Student Clubs

60+ Student Organizations

From competitive robotics to art collectives — there's a club for every passion. Or start your own.

EFREI Robotics Club

Design, build, and program autonomous robots. Compete in national and international robotics challenges like RoboCup and Eurobot.

💻

EFREI Dev Club

Weekly coding sessions, open-source contributions, and internal hackathons. Build real-world projects with fellow passionate developers.

🎮

E-Sports & Gaming

Compete in League of Legends, CS:GO, Valorant, and more. Our team has reached national university championships three years running.

🌱

GreenTech Initiative

Students working on sustainable technology projects, reducing campus carbon footprint, and advocating for responsible tech development.

🎨

Creative Arts & Media

Photography, graphic design, video production, and digital art. Collaborate on the annual student magazine and EFREI's YouTube channel.

🚀

Junior Enterprise

EFREI Junior Consulting is one of France's top junior enterprises. Work on real client projects and earn money while still studying.

🌍

International Students Union

Celebrate cultural diversity through international food fairs, language exchanges, and cultural events throughout the academic year.

Sports Associations

Academic Year

Key Events & Highlights

🏆
EFREI Hackathon October — 48 Hours

The biggest CS event of the year. 400+ participants, 72 hours, and €10,000 in prizes. Industry partners judge and recruit on the spot.

🎓
Career Fair (Forum EFREI) November — 2 Days

Over 200 companies attend to recruit interns, apprentices, and graduates. The largest engineering student forum in the Île-de-France region.

🌍
International Week February — 5 Days

Welcoming delegations from 25+ partner universities. Joint workshops, cultural immersion events, and global networking opportunities.

🎉
Graduation Ceremony June — Annual

A prestigious celebration honoring graduates in the Grand Amphitheatre. Keynote speakers from industry leaders and presentation of the best thesis awards.
