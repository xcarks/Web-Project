Work-Study Programs

Learn While You Earn

Alternance (apprenticeship) programs allow students to split time between EFREI and a partner company — gaining real experience while funding their studies.

🏢

How It Works

Students alternate between 3 days at EFREI and 2 days at their host company per week. Over the 2-year master's program, you accumulate substantial professional experience while receiving a salary that covers most educational expenses.

💼

Partner Companies

Our network includes 120+ companies from CAC 40 corporations to innovative startups, spanning banking, consulting, defense, e-commerce, and tech sectors.

BNP Paribas Capgemini Thales Atos Sopra Steria Amazon + 114 more
📈

Career Outcomes

Apprenticeship graduates typically receive a full-time offer from their host company.

Direct Hire Rate78%
Avg. Starting Salary (€)42K+

📋

Eligibility

Open to students enrolling in or already completing a Master's-level program at EFREI.

  • Valid work permit for France (EU or non-EU)
  • Age under 30 (standard) or up to 35 (special cases)
  • Minimum B2 French language level
  • Signed apprenticeship contract with a partner company
