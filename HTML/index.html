Welcome to our Department

The Computer Science Department at EFREI Paris is a dynamic hub for education and innovation, dedicated to training the next generation of engineers in a technology-driven world. The department combines strong theoretical foundations with hands-on experience in key areas such as software engineering, artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and distributed systems.

Over the years, the department has continuously evolved alongside the rapid advancements of the digital industry, adapting its programs to meet emerging technological challenges. Through close partnerships with leading companies and startups, students engage in real-world projects that enhance both their technical expertise and professional readiness.

The teaching approach emphasizes innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving. Through project-based learning, hackathons, and work-study programs, students develop not only technical skills but also essential soft skills required in today’s global tech environment.

Today, the department plays a key role in shaping highly skilled professionals, actively contributing to the digital ecosystem at both national and international levels.
